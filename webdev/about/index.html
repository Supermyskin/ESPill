About ESPill

ESPill's goal is to help:

  • The elderly
  • People with Alzheimer's/Dementia
  • And everyone in between

Our solution is:

We have developed a smart pill box that actively follows a customized, user-defined schedule. The hardware device consists of six separate compartments, each holding a different medication, which open automatically at their designated times. A crucial feature of this system is that if a user forgets to take their pill on time, the companion website instantly warns the user.

For this project we used:

  • ESP32
  • Arduino
  • A lot of sensors
  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • MQTT
